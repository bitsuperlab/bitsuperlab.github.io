Whatâ€™s BitSuperLab?

BitSuperLab is a non-profit research organization focusing on crypto currencies and DACs. Currently, the team is aiming to explore the wonderful BitShares world with experimental techniques, and committed to helping BTSers step into the DAC systems more efficiently via multiple solid products along with professional services, some of which you may have already been using. We develop services and tools to incubate BitShares delegates, and research on new DACs.

Actually, we are not limited to these stuffs, we have research on all kinds of crypto tech, and members are encouraged to do experiment on crypto area and invent new ideas and systems.

Current there are three program they focused on.

Delegate Incubator

The BitShares Delegates incubator programs, including operations tools, delegate hosting, etc.

BitShares Play

BitShares Play is an experiment to demonstrate and validate how a decentralized, autonomous game assets platform might be implemented. The platform features multiple provably -fair games of chance, as well as the ability to integrate various third party games and their asset systems. Shares of PLAY(PLS) are combined with all of the assets of both built-in and third party games form a free marketplace and exchange. Systems like this are also known as Decentralized Autonomous Companies (DAC).

LAB Asset Plan

LAB is the asset issued by BitSuperLab on BitShares X. We issued it to encouraging the BitSuperLab members to contribute as much as they can. It's the 'stake pool' that supports BitSuperLab on multiple projects aiming to help build the BitShares DACs world. LAB is one of the ways to attracting talents to be with BitSuperLab together exploring and serving, and also the investors seeing a profit as we are providing high quality products and services. LAB is also used to paying back to the friends who have helped BitSuperLab since all the stakes are possible to get dividends equally.
