DPOS Super Lab.

DPOS Super Lab is to join the competition of DPOS mining, because providing good service as a stable DPOS delegate is a challeging work, and could be very interesting and profitable.

Two goals:

  1. Act as one of the 100 delegate mining nodes, and keep stable with high reputation, 100% online.
  2. Testing DPOS network, reseach on all kinds of attack and cheating possibility. Find the flaw of other 99 mining nodes.

Three Principles:

  1. Bitsuperlab will only host up to 2 *active* third party delegates except Bitsuperlab, but unlimited positions for stand by delegates.
  2. If there is > 2 delegates becomes active, we will choose to keep only 2 delegates of them, others have to go independent, don't worry we will provide enough technical support for your migration util you are happy to run independently.
  3. Bitsuperlab will provide comprehensive consultant to your delegates here, our goal is to be the incubator of industry-level delegates for Bitshares community.

Quote from DPOS Mining Delegate Requirements

Becoming a delegate requires:

  1. Ability to run provided software on a low-latency connection
  2. Ability to setup a secure linux environment
  3. Ability to get 1% of the vote...
    • unique geographic location
    • trusted member of the community
    • 1000 BIPS (a couple of PTS or AGS) registration fee... (keep out spammers)

Remaining a delegate requires:

  + low latency, 99.9% uptime
  + non-discrimination of valid transactions
  + better reputation than all but the top 99

How to earn the best reputation:

  + Provide the most additional services for your pay.

        - seed node supporting thousands of connections
        - block explorers
        - free web wallets
        - high speed blockchain downloads

  + Provide the most proof as to your transparency

You get the idea... who ever is willing to provide the most service for their pay will likely win over someone attempting to provide the least service.
