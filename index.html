What’s BitSuperLab?

BitSuperLab is a non-profit research organization focusing on crypto currencies and DACs. Currently, the team is aiming to explore the wonderful BitShares world with experimental techniques, and committed to helping BTSers step into the DAC systems more efficiently via multiple solid products along with professional services, some of which you may have already been using. We develop services and tools to incubate BitShares delegates, and research on new DACs.

Actually, we are not limited to these stuffs, we have research on all kinds of crypto tech, and members are encouraged to do experiment on crypto area and invent new ideas and systems.
