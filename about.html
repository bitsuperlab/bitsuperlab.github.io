About Us

Bitsuperlab are group of people who are working on crypto assets and services, including development, operation and research. "LAB" asset in Bitshares X are the assets and equity of Bitsuperlab.

Our Hosting Services Principles

After careful consideration, to prevent hosting too many active delegates and making the delegates more decentralize, and help more standy by delegates to reduce there delegate running cost before get voted in top 101. Bitsuperlab choose following Three Principles.

  • Bitsuperlab will only host up to 2 *active* third party delegates except Bitsuperlab, but unlimited positions for stand by delegates.
  • If there are more than 2 delegates becomes active, we will choose to keep only 2 delegates of them, others have to go independent, don't worry we will provide enough technical support for your migration util you are happy to run independently.
  • Bitsuperlab will provide comprehensive consultant to your delegates here, our goal is to be the incubator of industry-level delegates for Bitshares community.
